ABOUT US

Our Passion



We are passionate about art and craft, and we want to share our passion with you.

We believe that everyone has an inner artist waiting to be unleashed, and we want to help you ignite your creativity and relive cherished memories through art and craft.

Whether you are a beginner or a pro, you will find everything you need to create amazing projects with Icraft.

We offer a wide range of craft materials, such as paints, brushes, stencils, stickers, flowers, resin, and more. You can browse through our online store and order your favorite products with just a few clicks.

We deliver your products to your doorstep in a timely and secure manner.

But we are more than just an online store. We are also a learning platform and a social media sensation. We have a blog, a newsletter, and a YouTube channel where we share our products, tutorials, tips, ideas, and examples of our art and craft projects. We also have a strong and loyal fan base on Instagram and Facebook, where we post daily updates, stories, reels, live sessions, and more. We showcase our work, interact with our followers, and feature their creations. We also host contests and giveaways to win exciting prizes and discounts.
